Ethical Considerations: Bias, Privacy, and Accountability

One of the primary ethical concerns surrounding AI is the issue of bias. This can lead to unfair or discriminatory outcomes in critical areas such as healthcare, where biased diagnostic tools could disproportionately affect certain demographic groups41. Data breaches and cyberattacks pose a constant threat to the privacy of individuals whose information is used to train and operate AI systems.
